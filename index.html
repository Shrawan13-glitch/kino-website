Features

Powerful Capabilities

Everything you need to manage your development workflow, right from your Android device.

🔧

On-Device Tool Calling

AI agents execute tools directly on your phone — no middleman server. Full control, full privacy.

Multi-Agent Support

Chain multiple AI agents together for complex automation pipelines and sophisticated workflows.

How It Works

Three Simple Steps

Get up and running in minutes, not hours.

01

Add Your API Key

Get an API key from OpenRouter or your preferred LLM provider. Paste it into Kino's settings — it never leaves your device.

02

Connect Your GitHub

Authenticate with your GitHub account. Kino uses secure OAuth tokens stored locally on your device.

03

Chat & Automate

Start chatting with your AI agent. Ask it to create repos, review PRs, manage files, run Actions — all in natural language.
